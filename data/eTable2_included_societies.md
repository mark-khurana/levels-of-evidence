# eTable 2. Included Societies and Coverage

Clinical practice guidelines from **77 professional societies** across **24 specialties** (536 guidelines, 22,877 graded recommendations) met inclusion criteria and were successfully extracted. Societies were identified through a single structured search (June 2026) combining PubMed [Practice Guideline] and ECRI/GIN registry queries, direct review of society websites, and a systematic audit across all specialties to capture societies publishing formally graded guidance within their own journals (84 candidate societies identified, 77 included).

## Identified but not obtained / excluded
- **Paywalled (identified, not obtained):** CCS, CHEST/ACCP, ESH.
- **Excluded a priori — non-English:** NHG (Dutch), DEGAM (German).
- **Excluded — strength-of-recommendation only (no per-recommendation level of evidence):** 17 guidelines (14 BAD, 1 ESE, BSAC, ESTES); see EXPANSION_METHODOLOGY.md.
